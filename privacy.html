Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Sortist ("the app") is a color-sorting puzzle game for iPhone. Your privacy is important. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and where it is stored.

Data We Collect

Sortist does not collect any personal information. The app does not require an account, does not track you, and does not send your data anywhere. There are no analytics SDKs, no advertising frameworks, and no third-party data collection of any kind.

Data Stored on Your Device

All game state — including your current level, settings, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Is removed if you delete the app

In-App Purchases

Sortist offers optional in-app purchases (Time Boost Pack and Remove Timer). All purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or process any payment information. Purchase records are managed by your Apple ID and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

Sortist does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporting libraries. The app makes no network requests during gameplay.

Children's Privacy

Sortist does not collect any personal data from anyone, including children under 13. The app contains no advertising and no data collection of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
