Simple to learn. Hard to put down.

Three moves. Infinite challenge. All you need is a quick tap.

1

Select

Tap any tube to select it. The top color segment lifts, ready to pour. Change your mind? Tap it again to deselect.

2

Pour

Tap another tube to pour. Colors can only pour onto a matching color or into an empty tube — think before you tap.

3

Sort

Fill each tube with a single color to complete the level. Every level adds more tubes and colors — the clock never stops.
